# ai-research-brief-generator

<div align="center">

**AI Research Brief Generator**

Automatically summarize market, competitor, and technology information into structured research briefs.

</div>

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project targets the workflow of AI research assistants who need to frequently process industry dynamics, competitor information, and technology trends. Manual collection is inefficient, and output formats are inconsistent.

The goal is to convert scattered information into standardized, report-ready research briefs.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📥 **Multi-source Input** - Support news, reports, documents
- 🔄 **Data Processing** - Cleaning, deduplication, categorization
- 📝 **Summary Generation** - Key points extraction
- 💡 **Recommendation Output** - Actionable suggestions and risk warnings
