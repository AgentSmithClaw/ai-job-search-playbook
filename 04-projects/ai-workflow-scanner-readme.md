<div align="center">

**AI Workflow Opportunity Scanner**

Identify AI-compatible business process scenarios and generate pilot proposals with ROI assessment.

</div>

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Many business teams know "we need to do AI", but don't know which process to start with or how to evaluate ROI.

This tool analyzes business process pain points, identifies AI-suitable scenarios, and outputs pilot recommendations.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Process Analysis** - Break down business processes into steps
- 📊 **Opportunity Scoring** - Evaluate based on value, feasibility, cost
- 📋 **Pilot Recommendations** - Generate actionable next steps
- 📈 **ROI Estimation** - Estimate implementation returns
